Camera Ready Instructions

Thank you very much for your contribution to our DMO-FinTech workshop at PAKDD 2024. The accepted submissions will be archived in Springer Workshop Proceedings (example1, example2).

Please read carefully the following instructions for preparing the camera-ready version. Please make sure to upload your camera-ready version in the submission system by Feb. 29th, 2024.
  • Please read the author’s guide provided by Springer via the following link.
    • Author’s Guide: https://www.springer.com/gp/computer-science/lncs/conference-proceedings-guidelines
    • Code of Conduct: https://www.springernature.com/gp/authors/book-authors-code-of-conduct
  • Full papers are limited to 12 pages, where short papers and industry abstracts are limited to 9 pages.
  • Please prepare all of your source files (LaTeX files with all the associated style files, special fonts and eps files, or Word files) in a folder and name the folder as your paper ID. Latex users: You should supply the bib and bbl files to avoid the omission of data during conversion from bib to bbl.
  • In addition, please prepare a final pdf of your paper and name it as “paper-ID.pdf.”
  • The corresponding author, please sign and date the License to Publish form LTP-xxx (docx / pdf), and scan it as a pdf. Please rename it as “LTP-ID.pdf.” Please note that the digital signature is not acceptable. Note: you can leave "title of proceedings" and "volume editors" blank in the form.
  • Finally, please zip the above folder and two pdf files as a zip file and name it as your “paperID.zip.” Then, upload the zip file in the EasyChair submission system.
  • Register the PAKDD conference or workshop, the early-bird deadline is March 10, 2024
For example, as shown in the following figure, if your paper ID is 666, you should have a folder named 666 containing all the source files and another “paper-666.pdf,” which is the final version of your paper. Additionally, a signed “LTP-666.pdf” by the corresponding author should be attached as well. You need to submit the zip file on EasyChair system.
